data DependencyType Source #

The type of a Dependency: normal, implicit, or order-only.

Since: 0.1.0

Constructors

NormalDependency

A normal dependency. These are listed in the $in variable and changes in the relevant target result in a rule execution.

Since: 0.1.0

OrderOnlyDependency

An order-only dependency. These are listed in the $in variable, but are only rebuilt if there is at least one non-order-only dependency that is out of date.

FIXME: double check this interpretation of the Ninja manual
